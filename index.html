About

    No Thrones is an html5 tactics-based rpg just starting development. Inspired by Valkyrie Profile: Covenant of the Plume, Final Fantasy Tactics Advance, and Fire Emblem, No Thrones aims to be the peak of the genre.

The name No Thrones is provisional.
